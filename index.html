Who We Are

About God's Grace Secondary School

Founded on the pillars of Excellence, Integrity, and Progress — we have been shaping the leaders of tomorrow since our establishment in Sango Ota.

Our Story

A Legacy of Excellence

God's Grace Secondary School was established with a singular vision: to provide world-class secondary education in the heart of Sango Ota. Over the years, we have grown from a small neighbourhood school into one of the most respected academic institutions in Ogun State.

Our name "GOD'S GRACE" symbolises our commitment to Fortitude, Excellence, Respect, Dedication, Enthusiasm, and Wisdom — values we instil in every student who passes through our gates.

2010
School Founded
God's Grace Secondary School was established in Sango Ota, Ogun State with an initial intake of 120 students from JSS1 to JSS3.
2013
Senior Secondary Opens
SSS1–SSS3 classes were launched, completing the full secondary school programme. First set of WAEC candidates recorded 88% pass rate.
2016
Boarding House Established
Separate male and female boarding houses were commissioned, accommodating students from across Ogun State and beyond.
2020
State Award for Excellence
Received the Ogun State Commissioner for Education Award for Outstanding Academic Performance in WAEC examinations.
2025
800+ Students, 92% WAEC Pass Rate
Today, GOD'S GRACE stands tall as one of the top-performing schools in Sango Ota, continuing to set the standard for excellence.

Entry Requirements

Who Can Apply?

For JSS1 Entry:

  • ✓ Primary School Leaving Certificate (PSLC)
  • ✓ Minimum of Credit in English & Mathematics
  • ✓ Age: 10–13 years (birth certificate required)
  • ✓ Pass the school's entrance examination

For SSS1 Entry:

  • ✓ BECE or JSS3 School Report Card
  • ✓ Minimum of 5 Distinctions in BECE subjects
  • ✓ Age: 14–17 years
  • ✓ Pass the school's entrance examination
